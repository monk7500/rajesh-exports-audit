About This Investigation

Methodology & Scope

This investigation was compiled from entirely public sources: SEBI regulatory orders, BSE/NSE exchange filings, REL's own annual reports (accessed directly from the company's S3 servers), MCA company records, Swiss regulatory disclosures, DRI press statements, Kerala court records, and credentialled investigative press.

The Verification Matrix independently grades each finding against a four-tier evidentiary standard: Verified (confirmed from primary records without relying on SEBI), Partially Verified (corroborated but incomplete), Unverifiable (SEBI allegation requiring forensic access), and New Finding (independently identified, not in prior SEBI order).

All allegations represent prima facie findings or investigative conclusions, not final judicial determinations. The company's right of response and due process is acknowledged throughout. This compilation is published for public interest, regulatory accountability, and journalistic reference purposes.
